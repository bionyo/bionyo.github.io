Ramen

This is the best recipe you will ever make!

Ingredients

  • Spicy Instant Ramen
  • Egg

Directions

  1. Wait until water boils
  2. Put ramen into water and boil for 5 minutes
  3. Stir in ramen flavoring and seasoning into pot
  4. After 5 minutes the noodles are firm lower head
  5. Strain remaining water then put back on stove
  6. Crack uncooked egg into ramen and stir until remains are cooked
